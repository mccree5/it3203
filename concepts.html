Key Concepts

  1. Semantic Structure: Appropriate elements convey meaning.
  2. Responsive Design: Layout adapts to viewport size.
  3. Color & Contrast: Ensure readable, accessible UIs.
  4. Typography: Hierarchy with headings, size, weight, and spacing.
  5. Grid vs Flex: 2D vs 1D layout decisions.
  6. Progressive Enhancement: Core content works without extras.
  7. Accessibility: Focus styles, alt text, and proper landmarks.
